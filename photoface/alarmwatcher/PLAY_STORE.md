# Play Store Metadata: Watchman Bridge

## App Title
**Watchman Bridge** (or **Watchman: Universal Alarm & DND Sync**)

## Short Description
Bridge phone alarms, critical alerts, and DND state to your Wear OS watch with per-app rules.

## Full Description
Watchman Bridge solves the "missing link" between your Android phone and Wear OS watch. It ensures you never miss a critical alert, even if your watch doesn't natively sync with your phone's brand.

### Key Features:
*   **Universal Alarm Sync:** Supports stock Android clocks, Samsung Clock, AMdroid, and more. When your phone rings, your watch rings. Support for remote Snooze/Dismiss included.
*   **Critical Alert Rules:** Create custom rules for high-priority apps (Ring, PagerDuty, Baby Monitors). Filter by sender or keyword and elevate notifications to full-screen watch alarms.
*   **Custom Sounds & Patterns:** Sync custom MP3 sounds and vibration rhythms from phone to watch for specific rules.
*   **Playback Duration:** Control exactly how long an alert rings on your watch (1-60s or "Until Dismissed").
*   **Universal DND Sync:** Sync Do Not Disturb and Bedtime state from your phone to your watch automatically, even across different brands (e.g., Pixel phone to Galaxy Watch).
*   **Smart Silence Bypass:** Grant permission once and let Watchman override your watch's silence for the alerts that truly matter.

### How it works:
1. Install Watchman Bridge on both your Phone and Wear OS watch.
2. Grant Notification Access on the phone.
3. Grant DND Access on the watch.
4. Configure your custom rules and volume levels.

Perfect for users of Galaxy Watch 4/5/6/7 with non-Samsung phones, or Pixel Watch users who want more control over their alerts.
